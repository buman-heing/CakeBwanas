✦ Our Story

About Cake Bwana's

A Zambian bakery born from passion, grown through trust, and dedicated to making every celebration sweeter.

Mwaiseni — Welcome

Lusaka's Artisan Bakery

Cake Bwana's Confectionery was founded with one unwavering belief: that every person — no matter the occasion — deserves a cake that is truly extraordinary. We are a registered Zambian business, proud to serve both our local community in Lusaka and our beloved diaspora customers who send love home through every order.

Our bakers are artisans. Each cake is hand-crafted, never rushed, never mass-produced. From the moment you place your order to the moment the candles are lit, we are with you every step of the way.

We serve Lusaka both through our physical premises and online via WhatsApp — making it effortlessly easy to order the perfect cake from anywhere in the world.

"Ukubombela abantu nge calo — to serve people is to serve the nation." We take that seriously.
